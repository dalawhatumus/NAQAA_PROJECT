About Naqaa Foundation

Naqaa Foundation is a non-profit organisation based in Cape Town, South Africa. The organisation was established to support communities through welfare projects, food distribution, education, and social upliftment initiatives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide hope, support, and practical assistance to individuals and families in need while promoting dignity, compassion, and unity.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create stronger communities where every person has access to basic needs, educational opportunities, and a supportive environment.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Community Service
  • Respect
  • Empowerment

Why Support Naqaa Foundation?

Through dedicated volunteers and community partnerships, Naqaa Foundation continues to make a positive impact through meaningful and sustainable programs.
