Our Services

Naqaa Foundation provides structured community development programs designed to respond to urgent needs while also creating long-term solutions for poverty, education gaps, and social challenges in local communities across Cape Town.

Our services are built around three main goals: reducing hunger, improving access to education, and strengthening community resilience through sustainable support systems.

All Programs & Initiatives

Food Relief

Weekly food parcels and hot meals distributed to families facing food insecurity across Cape Town. Emergency food support also available during crisis periods.

Education Support

Stationery, tutoring, school fee assistance, and mentorship programs aimed at improving learner performance and reducing dropout rates in the Western Cape.

Community Welfare

Clothing drives, household support, and emergency welfare initiatives to restore dignity and provide immediate relief to vulnerable individuals and families.

Volunteer Programs

Join our team on the ground. Volunteers assist in food distribution, event planning, outreach programs, fundraising, and administrative support across Cape Town.

Sponsorship & Donations

Support our programs financially or in-kind. Every contribution funds food programs, educational materials, and community services that reach hundreds of families monthly.

Spiritual Programs

Daily and Jumu'ah salah services, da'wah initiatives, and Islamic education programs providing spiritual support and guidance to the community.

Youth Development

Skills training, career guidance, and mentorship programs designed to prepare young people for future opportunities and build community leadership capacity.

Emergency Relief

Rapid response support for families experiencing sudden hardship, unemployment, or crisis situations — providing immediate food, clothing, and welfare assistance.
