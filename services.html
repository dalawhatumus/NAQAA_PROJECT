Our Services

Naqaa Foundation provides a range of community support services aimed at improving lives, assisting families in need, and promoting long-term development.

Food Relief Programs

We distribute food parcels, cooked meals, and emergency grocery support to vulnerable individuals and families in the community.

Educational Support

Our educational initiatives include school supplies, learning support, youth mentorship, and skills development opportunities.

Community Welfare

We assist with clothing drives, seasonal aid campaigns, and support for individuals facing financial hardship.

Volunteer Opportunities

Community members are encouraged to volunteer their time and skills in our events, outreach activities, and support programs.

Sponsorship and Donations

Businesses and individuals can sponsor projects or donate resources to help us expand our impact and reach more people in need.
