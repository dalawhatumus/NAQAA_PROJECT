Make an Enquiry

Complete the form below if you would like to volunteer, become a sponsor, make a donation, or request more information about Naqaa Foundation's community programs. We will respond to your enquiry within 2–3 business days.

How You Can Get Involved

Volunteer

Join our team on the ground. We need volunteers for food drives, education programs, and community events across Cape Town.

Sponsor

Partner with us as a business or individual sponsor. Your support directly funds our programs and helps us reach more families.

Donate

Every contribution counts. Donate food, clothing, school supplies, or funds to support our ongoing welfare programs.

General

Have a question or want to learn more? Send us a general enquiry and our team will get back to you as soon as possible.

What Happens After You Submit?

  1. We receive your enquiry — our team is notified immediately when your form is submitted.
  2. We review your details — we match your enquiry type to the right team member.
  3. We respond within 2–3 business days — you will receive a personalised response at the email address you provided.
  4. We get to work together — whether you volunteer, donate, or sponsor, we welcome you to the Naqaa family.
